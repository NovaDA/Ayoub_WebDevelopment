GHOST HUNTERS

Paranormal Testimonies was created to create a place where users with a love of the paranormal, scary stories, UFO sightings could form a community. The platform provides uses with the ability to log UFO sightings on a map, write user-generated stories on a blog, alongside commenting and reacting to stories. Paranormal Testimonies is a website designed for those who want to share their stories with the supernatural. Each page of the website is designed as a repository of these stories according to three subject matters: Ghostly apparitions, extra-terrestrial encounters, and cryptid sightings. Users browsing the website can access the stories corresponding to their particular subject of interest via the navigation bar. If they choose to do so, they can also share their own experience by filling out the form that appears onscreen after clicking on the "SHARE YOUR STORY" button.
